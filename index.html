About Us

With over 14 years of experience in sports recruitment, SFK Sport Group has established itself as a premier agency for identifying and nurturing football talent in Ethiopia. Our comprehensive scouting network reaches every region of the country.

Our player development methodology combines technical training with mental preparation, ensuring our athletes are prepared for the demands of professional football. We provide personalized development plans tailored to each player's unique strengths and areas for improvement.

Beyond recruitment, we're committed to making a positive impact in communities across Ethiopia through football programs, training facilities, and educational initiatives that empower young athletes.
